About the College

Year of Establishment: 1958

Brief History: H. K. E. Society's Sir M Visvesvaraya College of Engineering (formerly SLN College of Engineering) is run by the H. K. E. Society of Kalaburgi. The Hyderabad Karnataka Education Society, popularly known as HKE Society, was established in 1958 by Late Sri. Mahadevappa Rampure at Gulbarga. The Society is dedicated to providing quality education in backward areas, ensuring that students receive facilities comparable to those in more developed regions.

Principal's Message:
